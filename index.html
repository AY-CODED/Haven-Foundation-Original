Our Mission

To provide shelter, education, and holistic support to young women and children facing adversity, with the aim of breaking cycles of poverty, abuse, and neglect.

What We Do

Safe Haven & Shelter: We provide a secure, welcoming home for those who have been displaced, orphaned, or are escaping harmful environments. Education & Skill Building: Through academic programs, vocational training, and mentorship, we equip young women and children with the tools they need for a brighter future. Health & Wellness: We offer access to healthcare, counseling, and nutrition programs to promote physical and emotional well-being. Advocacy & Awareness: We raise awareness about the rights of women and children and work toward systemic change in communities.

Our Vision

A world where every young woman and child has the opportunity to live in safety, receive a quality education, and grow into confident, self-reliant individuals.

About Us

Young Mother & Child Haven Foundation is a faith-driven non-governmental organization committed to nurturing, empowering, and educating young mothers, girls, and children.

We exist to offer hope, healing, and holistic development to those facing the challenges of early motherhood, unwanted pregnancies, and lack of guidance. Our mission is to transform lives through spiritual support, emotional healing, life skills development, and educational outreach.

We believe every young mother and child deserves a safe space to grow, thrive, and embrace a purposeful future. Through school outreaches, community programs, mentorship, and faith-based teachings, we address real issues affecting girls and young mothers — from sexual education and teenage pregnancy prevention to mental health support and vocational training.

Our goal is to break cycles of shame, abandonment, and ignorance by building a community rooted in hope, love, and restoration.

At Young Mother & Child Haven Foundation, we see potential where others see problems. We walk with every girl and young mother, reminding them: You are not alone. You are loved. Your story can be beautiful.
